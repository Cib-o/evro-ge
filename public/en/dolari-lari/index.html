Dollar rate in GEL today

On this page, you will find today's official USD to GEL exchange rate — i.e., how much 1 USD is worth today. Data comes from the National Bank of Georgia and updates automatically, so the dollar rate (dolaris kursi) is always current. Use the converter above to calculate any amount — enter the USD amount to see how much GEL it is, or vice versa.

How the official dollar rate is determined

The National Bank sets the official (indicative) USD rate every business day based on interbank trading, and it takes effect the next day. This is not the price at which you buy or sell dollars directly — commercial banks and exchange offices set their own buy/sell rates, which differ slightly from the official one.

How much is 1 USD in GEL and where to exchange it?

If you are wondering how much 1 USD is in GEL or how much 100 USD will cost, first check the official rate shown above, then — before you actually exchange — compare the rates of several banks and exchange offices. The difference may seem small, but it is noticeable on large amounts. Often, specific amounts have their own pages, such as 100 USD to GEL or 500 USD to GEL.

What affects the USD exchange rate?

The USD/GEL exchange rate changes due to global factors (strength of the US dollar in the global market, oil prices) as well as local reasons — tourism season, remittances, export-import balance, and National Bank interventions. That is why it is worth tracking the rate if you regularly transfer or receive USD.
