Ruble rate in GEL today

On this page, you will find today's official ruble (RUB) rate in GEL — i.e., how much 1 ruble is worth today. Data comes from the National Bank of Georgia and is updated automatically, so the ruble rate (rublis kursi) is always current. You can convert any amount to GEL or vice versa using the converter.

The ruble rate is often relevant for money transfers. The National Bank sets the official ruble rate every business day, while the rate in banks and exchange offices may differ slightly. See also Currency exchange rate and EUR rate.
